Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using the app?

Simply download the app, sign up for a free trial, and start asking questions about your iPhone. You can use either text or voice input to ask your questions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > iPhone Help, then tap "Cancel Subscription". Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

What happens after my free trial?

After the 7-day free trial, your subscription will automatically continue at $2.99 per month unless cancelled. You can cancel anytime during the trial period.

How do I enable voice input?

Tap the microphone icon and grant microphone permissions when prompted. Voice input requires iOS 15.0 or later.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your conversation history is stored locally on your device, and we don't share any personally identifiable information.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you've reinstalled the app or switched devices, tap the "Restore Purchases" button in the app's settings to restore your active subscription.

Troubleshooting

  • App not responding: Force close the app and restart it. If the issue persists, restart your device.
  • Voice input not working: Check your microphone permissions in Settings > iPhone Help > Microphone.
  • Subscription issues: Verify your subscription status in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
  • Network errors: Check your internet connection and try again. The app requires an active internet connection.

System Requirements

  • iOS 15.0 or later
  • Compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch
  • Internet connection required
  • Microphone access required for voice input
